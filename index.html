We're more connected than ever — and somehow further apart.

Life gets full. You think of someone you care about, but you never quite know if it's a good time to call — so you don't. Weeks slip by. Months.

And too many of us, when a hard moment comes, aren't sure who we'd even pick up the phone to call. Porch Light was built to change that.

Three simple ways to stay connected

Porch Light

A standing invitation to reach you.

Turn your light on when you're free to talk, and your friends can see you're around. Reaching out never feels like an interruption — no more guessing, no more bad timing.

Auto Call

Don't let the months slip by.

Good intentions fade. Auto Call sets up a recurring conversation at a frequency you choose and rings you both when you're available — so staying close actually happens, instead of slipping away.

Knock

Someone to call, right now.

Some moments can't wait. One tap on Knock connects you with someone who cares, within minutes — because everyone deserves to have someone to call.

No posts. No likes. No strangers.

Porch Light isn't social media. There's no feed to perform for, no audience, no followers to chase. Just genuine, one-on-one conversation with the handful of people who actually matter to you.
