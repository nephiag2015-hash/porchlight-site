Porch Light

Keep the light on.

Spontaneous phone calls with the people who matter — no scheduling, no endless texting. Turn your light on when you're free, and let friends knock.

Download on the App Store Coming soon to Google Play

Remember just knocking on a friend's door?

Porch Light brings that back. When you're free to talk, you turn your porch light on. Friends can see who's around and send a "knock." Answer it and you're instantly on a call. When your light's off, no one bothers you — simple as that.

How it works

1

Turn your light on

One tap signals to your friends that you're free for a call right now.

2

Get a knock

A friend taps your light, and your phone rings. No texting back and forth to find a good time.

3

Just talk

Pick up for a real voice call. Spontaneous, low-pressure, and actually human.

What's inside

Availability signals

Your porch light shows friends exactly when you're open to a call — and hides you when you're not.

Knocks & calls

Tap a friend's light to ring them. Real voice calls, connected right inside the app.

Circles

Group your people — close friends, family, the crew — so the right folks know you're around.

Auto Porch Light

Your light turns on when you get home and off when you're driving or in a meeting — automatically.

Quiet hours

Set the times you never want to be disturbed. Your light stays off, no exceptions.

Private by design

Location stays on your device. No call recordings. We never sell your data.

Why Porch Light?

Texting was supposed to bring us closer, but somewhere along the way the spontaneous phone call disappeared. We schedule everything, overthink a simple "you free?", and end up never calling at all.

Porch Light makes it effortless again — a quiet signal that you've got a minute to connect, and a friend on the other end who's glad you're around.
