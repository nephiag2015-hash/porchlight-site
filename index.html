We're more connected than ever — and somehow further apart.

Life gets full. You think of someone you care about, but you never quite know if it's a good time to call — so you don't. Weeks slip by. Months.

And too many of us, when a hard moment comes, aren't sure who we'd even pick up the phone to call. Porch Light was built to change that.

Three simple ways to stay connected

Porch Light

Always on at the right moments.

Your porch light is a standing invitation — when it's on, your people know it's a good time to call. And you barely have to think about it: Porch Light turns your light on and off for you automatically, around your day, so you're reachable when it makes sense and never when it doesn't.

Auto Call

Don't let the months slip by.

Good intentions fade. Auto Call sets up a recurring conversation at a frequency you choose and rings you both when you're available — so staying close actually happens, instead of slipping away.

Knock

Someone to call, right now.

Tap Knock and Porch Light rings everyone whose light is on — one after another, until someone picks up — then drops you straight into a call. No "you up?" texts, no waiting around. A real conversation, in minutes.

No posts. No likes. No strangers.

Porch Light isn't social media. There's no feed to perform for, no audience, no followers to chase. Just genuine, one-on-one conversation with the handful of people who actually matter to you.

Built for real life

Home base

Your light comes on the moment you get home — so your people know you've landed and you're free to talk. Your location never leaves your phone.

Quiet hours

Block out dinner, bedtime, the moments that are yours. Your light stays dark and nothing comes through — so downtime stays downtime.

Calendar

Soon, your light will dim itself when your calendar's busy — so a call never pulls you out of a meeting.

Driving

Soon, your light will switch on for hands-free drives — turning your commute into time to actually catch up.
